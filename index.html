How To PLay:

Pong is a game where you play alone against a computer. You move your paddle to try and stop the ball from going past you, if the ball goes past you the computer gets 1 point, the same goes for you. if the ball goes past the computer you get 1 point. The score will be centered on the top of the board, to move your paddle you have to move the cursor up & down, Have Fun.
